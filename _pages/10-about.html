I am Lazarus Lazaridis, I am a developer and I live in Athens, Greece.

My weekends start with 

mkdir best_idea_ever

and end with 

sudo rm -rf ./best_idea_ever

I usually code in Ruby especially when it's on Rails but I also speak Java, Go, bash & C#.

I love open source and I like writing tutorials and creating tools and utilities.

I write my posts thinking how I would prefer to have the information in order to understand what is going on. My way of writing, of course, does not work for everybody so at the end of each post you will find a GitHub issue for feedback/suggestions/typos etc. I'm always grateful for your feedback.

I love animals, cats are my favorite, most of my posts end with a cat photo.

I am writing my posts with this on the side.
